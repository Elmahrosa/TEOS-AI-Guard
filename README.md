## ðŸ›¡ Compliance Guardrails

- **DSPM (Data Security Posture Management)**  
  Discover and classify data before AI sees it.

- **DLP (Data Loss Prevention)**  
  Enforce one policy across cloud, web, email, endpoints, and APIs.

- **DDR (Data Detection & Response)**  
  Continuous monitoring, threat detection, and incident readiness.

- **Audit Logs**  
  Tamper-proof, privacy-preserving, regulator-ready records.

- **Jurisdiction Awareness**  
  AI behavior adapts by country, sector, and regulatory context.
